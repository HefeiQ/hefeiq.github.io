CS Seminar
CS Seminar
2018 fall: Lecturer

The CS Seminar offers academic support to students enrolled in various computer science courses (e.g., CS210, CS310, CS420) and follows the program of study outlined in each syllabus. It offers individual and small-group tutoring that is tailored to specific student needs.
